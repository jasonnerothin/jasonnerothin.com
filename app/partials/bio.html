Jason was born in the mid-1970s, in rural mid-Minnesota, the first-born child of a physician and nurse. A sister and brother came 2 and 5 years later on.

Jason matriculated to St.Olaf College where he pursued a number of interesting studies - music, German, biology and - eventually - chemistry. After graduation, Jason set off for Ann Arbor, Michigan, supporting his continued studies at Eastern Michigan University with a short and undistinguished career as a bench scientist.

By the end of his twenties, Jason had attained another two degrees and gotten married to Shelley. Crucially, he was being paid to do work with computers, mostly of the “coding” variety.

After a two-year stint in southern California and the addition of a child, the family relocated to Madison, Wisconsin, where they were again granted the blessings of parenthood twice more. A grey tabby - Whatserface - rounds out the current mix.

Jason has far more interests than time. Since graduating college, he's picked up a new hobby about once every other year: Cooking, ruffed grouse hunting, Philip Ward's Lifetime Of Reading, fixin’ old trucks, gardening, money management, and singing in his church choir are all going concerns.

Shelley and Jason own and operate a software company, Superior Biomining LLC, from which Jason consults on an independent basis. However, Whatserface is rumored to be the one pulling all the strings.
